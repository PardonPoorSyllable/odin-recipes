primavera pasta!!

truly a legendary dish… although it wasn't as healthy and balanced when it was the only meal consumed
throughout the day, it was delicious all the way!

ingredients could vary, sorts of pasta might differ, but one thing remained always: it. was. very. tasty.

Ingredients

  • onions, multi-layered
  • carrots; sticks aren't necessary
  • zucchini — I had to look up that word in English
  • bell or cayenne pepper, not porsche
  • tomatoes
  • salt, condiments, bay leaf
  • greens!!
  • last but not least, pasta!!

Steps

  1. slice 'em onions!!
  2. warm the pan up so that onions would fry
  3. slice 'em carrtos!! in rings
  4. fry it fry it fry it
  5. add a little of oil as well
  6. wash, peel, and slice zucchini in half!!
  7. cut the halves in thirds, then get thin slices
  8. not too thin, like 4mm
  9. throw the zucchini when onions are soft
  10. slice the pepper and tomatoes!!
  11. a-and that's too much already, figure the rest yourself
